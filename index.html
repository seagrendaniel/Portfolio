About Me

I am an entreprenurial engineer with a background in hardware and software development. I have always had a passion for beautiful design. I have over 5 years experience in bringing hardware and software products from inception to final production and market integration.

After graduating from Columbia University in May 2014, I founded Summerboard Inc, a business that designed and patented the first ever 'electric snowboard', an electric skateboard that allows the rider to emulate the mechanics of snowboarding on pavement by enabling the user to ride forwards, backwards, and sideways up to 25mph using tandem-rotating powered caster wheels. I continued to pursue my love of software engineering during this time - optimizing the motor control algorithms for our boards and discovering my love of website and web application design.
